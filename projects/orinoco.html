About this project

Orinoco is an e-commerce furniture website. Articles are presented on the home page, the user can click on an article to learn more about it. If he is interested, he can add the article to his basket by having previously chosen the color of the desired article. The user can then continue shopping or proceed to payment. He will find on the basket page the list of his articles where he can modify the quantity or delete them. After having checked his basket, the user can enter his payment information in the form on the right, he will then be redirected to a confirmation page where his order id will be displayed.

By carrying out this project, I was introduced to javascript and in particular to API calls and the use of local storage. I also learned a lot about web design by creating the wireframe and the graphic charter for this project.
